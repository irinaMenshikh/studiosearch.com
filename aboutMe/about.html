About Studio SEarch

We are Studio SEarch, a boutique interior design firm in Istanbul. For 20 years experience we have been creating interior design solutions, and we are proud to have worked in such great hotel & congress hall project & residence projects. We provide a range of services for businesses and individuals, these generally include (but are not limited to) interior design for residential and commercial property projects like hotels, residences, restaurants, bars, gyms, conference halls & offices and private houses. Hotel & congress hall & residential complexes & private residences interior design background for 20 years with various international brands such as Fairmont, Le Meridien, Sheraton, Swissotel , Doubletreee by Hilton, Hilton, Emaar The Address Hotel , Titanic Hotels etc. At studio SEarch, we seek to redefine the creation by inspiring, enriching, and engaging the human experience & soul. We are designing places for creating inspring places that will excite, welcome, please and satisfy. We explore new and innovative solutions to design the space we want to live in. A collection of our creative endeavours, interior design, concept development, lighting design, product design, signage design and art consultant. We are passionate!
