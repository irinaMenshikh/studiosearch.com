Experience

Over the years, the studio has designed many hotels with different flagships and in a number of countries, reflecting various cultures. Early on, Studio SEarch learned the importance of proper sequencing of the design process to insure successful implementations of the projects.

2

Knowledge and understanding of the hotel industry

This knowledge was acquired working for the industry. First, by working in hotels, members of the team at Studio Search learned to understand operational requirements and needs. Based on the latter, the knowledge is articulated to the design development process, benefiting the owners, the investors and the brands, which are secured knowing that professionals are handling their projects. In addition, by frequently attending industry events, shows, investment conferences, travelling and staying in hotels in many different geographical locations, the knowledge is kept current and updated to reflect the evolving requirements of this industry and that of a younger generation, whose lifestyle is affected very much by the use of technology and social media.

For over three decades Studio SEarch has delivered many hotel projects reflecting the visions of their owners, while respecting and integrating the procedures and standards of the brands.
